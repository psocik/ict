## Anthropic Expands Access to Claude Mythos Preview 🚀

Anthropic has expanded access to **Claude Mythos Preview**, its groundbreaking model that could potentially redefine cybersecurity as we know it! 🌐 This expansion is part of their **Project Glasswing** initiative, making the model available to **150 new organizations** across **15 countries**.

### New Partnerships 🤝

The new partner organizations span various industries, including:
- Power
- Water
- Healthcare
- Communications
- Hardware

These partners maintain critical codebases relied upon by numerous organizations worldwide, including governments. They are also potential targets for **catastrophic cyberattacks**. According to Anthropic, a significant cyberattack against any of these organizations could impact over **100 million people** and have serious implications for national and global security.

### Testing Security Vulnerabilities 🔍

The access granted to these organizations will allow them to use the model in a limited capacity to test and identify security vulnerabilities, ensuring they can be patched before being exploited by malicious actors.

### Insights from Cloudflare ☁️

Cloudflare has noted that **Mythos Preview** excels at exploit chain construction, which involves identifying how multiple bugs can be combined to create more damaging attacks. However, Anthropic has also acknowledged that **Mythos** is not yet ready for widespread use. The model's organic safeguards can be inconsistent, and while it is proficient at spotting vulnerabilities, it struggles with patching them effectively.

### Conclusion 🔒

Some cybersecurity experts have raised concerns that keeping the model under wraps does not address the broader issue of security vulnerabilities; it merely gives a select few a head start. This situation reflects a modern interpretation of **"security through obscurity,"** a method criticized in contemporary cybersecurity practices.
