## Information Sought on UNC5792 Cyber Group

🚨 **Rewards for Justice (RFJ)** is actively seeking information on **UNC5792**, a malicious cyber group linked to the **Russian Federal Security Service (FSB)** Border Guards. This group has been involved in extensive phishing campaigns targeting **Signal** and **WhatsApp** accounts of U.S. government officials, military leaders, and allied personnel.

### Malicious Activities

Using social engineering techniques, these cyber actors exploit legitimate device-linking features in secure messaging applications to gain unauthorized access to sensitive communications. After compromising an account, they can send messages and conduct further phishing attacks against other accounts using the same applications. In some cases, UNC5792 has altered legitimate "group invite" pages to redirect users to malicious URLs, linking a UNC5792-controlled device to the victim's Signal account. Although these activities do not exploit any security vulnerabilities in the platforms' encryption, they have compromised thousands of accounts.

### Target Audience

The targets of this cyber scheme include:
- U.S. government officials
- Diplomatic personnel
- Defense and national security personnel
- Policy analysts and advisors
- NATO member-state officials and diplomats
- Investigative journalists covering Russia, Ukraine, and international affairs
- NGOs supporting Ukraine
- Academic researchers in security studies and Russian affairs

### Call for Information

RFJ seeks detailed information on UNC5792 actors, including:
- Names, locations, and biographical details
- Affiliations within Russian intelligence services
- Identities of personnel providing technical support
- Contractors or third-party entities involved
- Domain names, server locations, and hosting providers
- Financial accounts and cryptocurrency wallets
