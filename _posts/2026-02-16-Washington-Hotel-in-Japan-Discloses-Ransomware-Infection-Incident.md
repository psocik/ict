The **Washington Hotel**, a brand under **Fujita Kanko Inc.**, has reported a ransomware attack that compromised its servers, exposing various business data. With **30 locations** across Japan and nearly **5 million guests** annually, this incident raises significant concerns for the hospitality industry.

### Key Details:
- **Date of Breach:** February 13, 2026, at 22:00 (local time)
- **Immediate Action:** IT staff disconnected servers from the internet to contain the attack.
- **Investigation:** An internal task force has been established, and external cybersecurity experts are assessing the situation.

### Impact on Operations:
The attack has affected operations at some properties, including the temporary unavailability of credit card terminals. However, the company assures that customer data is likely safe, as it is stored on separate servers managed by another company.

### Ongoing Investigation:
While the investigation continues, no ransomware groups have claimed responsibility for the attack as of now. The financial impact is still under review, and updates will be provided as more information becomes available.
