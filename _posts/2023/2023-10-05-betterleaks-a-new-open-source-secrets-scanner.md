## Introducing Betterleaks 🚀

We are excited to announce **Betterleaks**, a new open-source secrets scanner designed to replace Gitleaks! This innovative tool aims to enhance your security by scanning for sensitive information in your codebase.

### Key Features:
- **Open-Source**: Fully transparent and community-driven.
- **Enhanced Scanning**: Improved algorithms for better detection of secrets.
- **User-Friendly**: Easy to integrate into your existing workflows.

Stay ahead of potential security risks with Betterleaks!
